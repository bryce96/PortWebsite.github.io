Introduction

I am a recent Information Systems graduate looking to grow my expertise in the field. As a previous worker in the service industry, I'm highly comfortable delivering satisfactory results in a demanding team environment. As a current Helpdesk Analyst at Abbvie Pharmaceuticals, it is my responsibility to ensure that all our users are offered some form of resolution to their IT needs. Even when handling issues outside my scope, I am still able to use the information I receive to affect a logical course of action for the issue's management. This incident handling process extends over multiple systems across 2 different companies. I have also been highly passionate about technology for most of my life. As a supplement to my academic pursuits, I've achieved Google's IT Professional Support Certification, and continue to work to increase my education/knowledge via training programs available on Udemy, Microsoft Learn, Trailhead, etc. Below, you will find a collection of the information systems projects I have worked to refine over the years. My goal is to help companies achieve maximum informational efficiency through effective database design, database administration, data analytics, and data manipulation. I understand that each business will have different sets of requirements regarding the way their data is handled, and I aim to emulate the skills/qualities necessary to exceed those expectations.
